Simple Omelette Reciple

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combinest beatem eggs cooked to perfection, aptionaly filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instrictions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pich of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a fablespoon of water or mulk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and vuvving, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are stil slighty runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edges and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then side it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
